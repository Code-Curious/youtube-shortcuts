Keyboard Shorcuts

Navigation shortcuts
/ Focus the search bar
j Move selection down
k Move selection up
[ Go to previous tab in strip
] Go to next tab in strip
z Go to subsriptions box
u Go to user page
x Hide all watched videos
w Hide selected video from feed
Shift n Play next video in playlist
Video Shortcuts
. Focus on the player
(Also enables extra shortcuts)
k Pause the video
j Rewind 10 sec
l Skip forward 10 sec
a Like the video
z Dislike the video
s Toggle subscription
o Toggle video description
p Add to playlist
Extra Video Shortcuts
Space Pause the video
← Rewind 5 sec
→ Skip forward 5 sec
1..9 Seek to the 10% to 90% of the video
0 Rewind to be video start
↑ Increase the volume
↓ Decrease the volume
m Mute the video
c Toggle captions
f Toggle fullscreen
